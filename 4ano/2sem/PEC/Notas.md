## Governança, Processos e ISO 42001 em Sistemas de IA
- [ISO 42001](https://www.iso.org/standard/83123.html)
- [ISO 42001:2024 - Artificial Intelligence Management Systems](https://www.iso.org/standard/83123.html)

Os [Big5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Five_personality_traits):


- Conscientiousness: measures **self-control**, **diligence**, and **attention** to detail.
- Agreeableness: measures **kindness**, **helpfulness**, and **willingness** to cooperate.
- Neuroticism: measures **depression**, **irritability**, and **proneness** to anxiety.
- Openness: measures **creativity**, **curiosity**, and **willingness** to entertain new ideas.
- Extraversion: measures **boldness**, **energy**, and **social interactivity**.

Com IA não podemos avaliar a personalidade de uma pessoa, atribuindo valores a ela mesmo conforme os comportamentos da mesma.
